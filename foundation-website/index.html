About Us

United in Compassion, Changing Young Lives

Hope for Kids Care Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged children through education, healthcare, nutrition, and holistic development programs. We believe every child deserves a chance to dream, learn, and grow.

Founded with the vision of creating a child-friendly India, we work closely with communities, schools, and government bodies to ensure that no child is left behind.

Registered under 12A & 80G
NITI Aayog Registered
100% Transparent Operations
CSR-1 Certified
